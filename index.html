Welcome to Connect or Not to Connect

Instruction

In this game, you are required to connect one color to the same color without crossing another color, the player have to click on the main 2 existing colors to select other blocks,all blocks have to be colored to clear the level and move on, there will be a block that will show you what color you have selected

PLEASE BE AWARE IF YOU GO BACK A LEVEL YOU WILL NEED TO BEAT THE LEVEL AGAIN TO CONTINUE
